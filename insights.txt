Regional Performance

Business Recommendations
- Avoid discounts greater than 20% unless clearing old inventory.
- Focus marketing efforts on top-performing products.
- Reevaluate pricing strategy in low-profit regions.
- Prepare inventory in advance for seasonal demand peaks.

Conclusion
This analysis demonstrates how pricing strategy, product performance, and regional demand influence business profitability and growth.
