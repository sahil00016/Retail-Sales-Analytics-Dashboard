## Project Overview
This project analyzes a retail sales dataset using Power BI to identify trends, profitability drivers, and regional performance insights. The goal is to transform raw transactional data into actionable business intelligence through interactive visual dashboards.

---

## Objective
To evaluate sales performance and provide data-driven insights that can support strategic business decision-making.

---

## Dashboard Features
- KPI summary cards for Revenue, Profit, and Orders
- Monthly sales trend analysis
- Category-wise revenue comparison
- Regional performance distribution
- Discount vs Profit relationship analysis
- Interactive filtering capability

---

## Key Insights
- Revenue exceeded $2.29M across all transactions.
- Higher discounts significantly reduce profitability.
- Technology category generates the highest revenue.
- Certain products dominate overall sales contribution.
- Sales patterns indicate seasonal demand fluctuations.
